Assumptions for the analysis

The occurrence in the charts serves as a measure of popularity for the evaluation. We therefore assume that the song was among the most popular songs if it achieved a placement in the year-end charts. This assumption serves as the basis for the analysis and evaluation of the data.

In addition, attempts at explanation can be found in the evaluation. These often contain assumptions and are not based on further data if this is not explicitly stated. The explanation only suggests an interpretation of the results.
