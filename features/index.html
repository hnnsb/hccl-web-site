How did the Features change over time?

Songs are getting shorter and shorter.

We have received the data about the duration of the songs as audio features. Shown here are the averages of the different years in seconds. It is clear that the duration of the songs increased from 1970 to the peak in 1990. This can be justified by the fact that 50 years ago the production of songs was associated with higher costs. From 1990 onwards, it became clear that, despite the presumably now low costs involved in music production, the duration was declining. For the year 2019, the average duration of 198 seconds was below the limit of 200 seconds for the first time since 1970. This can probably be justified from two perspectives. On the one hand, the attention span is becoming shorter and shorter, which is also evident on platforms with high awareness such as TikTok. On the other hand, the shorter song duration could contribute to a song being heard more frequently as a result. So if billing is based on views rather than purchases, companies and artists will benefit more from shorter songs.
