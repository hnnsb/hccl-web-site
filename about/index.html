Who are we?

The HCCL group consists of four members. Hannes, Constantin, Christian and Leon. From the first letters also the group name has resulted. We all study "Wirtschaftsinformatik" in the 5th semester and attend the Data Science project as part of our studies. We all enjoy listening to music and have been interested in the changes in musical tastes over time.

What do we do?

Within the Data Science project we deal with the data, which are described under "Data". The goal was to generate information from these data by means of suitable evaluation and processing, to what extent the known music has changed over time.
